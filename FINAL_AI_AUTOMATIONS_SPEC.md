# Final AI Automation Specification - Based on User Requirements

## Core Categories (6 Main Areas)

### 1. Guest Experience & Front Desk Automation
**Goal**: Create "wow" moments while reducing staff workload

**Automations**:
1. AI Concierge / Chatbot - 24/7 multilingual via WhatsApp, website, or kiosk
2. Smart Check-in & Check-out - ID verification, digital keys, payment automation
3. Automated Upselling - AI suggests upgrades at optimal times
4. Personalized Itineraries - AI curates activities based on preferences
5. Guest Feedback & Review Automation - Sentiment analysis, flag issues before bad reviews

### 2. Operations & Housekeeping Automation
**Goal**: Operational efficiency for large resorts

**Automations**:
1. Room Readiness & Assignment - Sync cleaning schedules with check-in times
2. Inventory & Supply Alerts - AI forecasts linens, amenities, minibar stock
3. Maintenance Requests - Predictive alerts (AC, lights, equipment)
4. Task Assignment Bots - Auto-assign tasks by occupancy and urgency

### 3. Marketing & CRM Automation
**Goal**: Boost revenue and loyalty with personalized engagement

**Automations**:
1. AI Guest Segmentation - Group by behavior, spending, preferences
2. Email & SMS Campaigns - Abandoned bookings, offers, birthdays
3. Dynamic Pricing Automation - Adjust rates by occupancy, demand, competitors
4. Loyalty & Re-engagement Triggers - Automated outreach to past guests

### 4. Communication & Multichannel Automation
**Goal**: Fast, human-like responses everywhere

**Automations**:
1. Omnichannel Inbox - Centralize WhatsApp, Messenger, email
2. AI Auto-Responder - FAQs instantly, route complex cases
3. Voice Assistant Integration - Room service, housekeeping (Alexa/Google)

### 5. Management & Analytics Automation
**Goal**: Give decision-makers visibility and control

**Automations**:
1. AI Dashboard - Real-time KPIs (occupancy, revenue, satisfaction, energy)
2. Forecasting Models - Predict bookings, staffing, maintenance
3. Expense Optimization - Identify unnecessary costs
4. Sustainability Monitoring - Track water, energy, waste

### 6. Integration & Custom Automation
**Goal**: Flexibility connecting to existing systems

**Features**:
- PMS integration (Opera, Cloudbeds, Mews)
- CRM, POS, accounting, HR systems
- Smart devices and IoT sensors
- Custom dashboards and workflow bots

## Implementation Notes

Each automation includes:
- `id`: Unique identifier
- `name`: Clear automation name
- `type`: time | revenue | conversion
- `savings`: Realistic percentage (research-backed)
- `desc`: Short description
- `metrics`: Which sliders to show
- `whatItDoes`: Technical explanation
- `howItHelps`: Business benefits
- `realWorld`: Case study or benchmark
- `stats`: Two key metrics

All automations focus on:
1. Creating "wow" moments for guests
2. Reducing staff workload
3. Increasing revenue through intelligence
4. Providing real-time visibility
5. Integrating with existing systems
